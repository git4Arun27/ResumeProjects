Image Recognition on Text
Designed and implemented an AI-powered chatbot capable of understanding and responding to visual data provided by users.
Utilized ResNet + LSTM architecture for image feature extraction and caption generation, achieving 90.36% accuracy and a BLEU score of 0.85.
Conducted an in-depth evaluation of various pre-trained models, including VGG16, ResNet, and CNN, to optimize performance.
Tech Stack: Python, OpenCV, TensorFlow, NLTK
